The campus caravan tourism initiative, alongside the peace forum, offers a unique platform to promote cultural exchange, tourism, and community building among students and local communities. this article explores how these initiatives foster peace, understanding, and sustainable tourism.

Campus caravan tourism is a dynamic program designed to engage students in exploring diverse cultures and promoting sustainable travel practices. this initiative encourages students to travel to various campuses and communities, learning about local traditions, history, and social issues. by immersing themselves in different environments, participants gain a deeper understanding of global perspectives and challenges.

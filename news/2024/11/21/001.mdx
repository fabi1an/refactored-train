Goldenstate College is proud to announce that its midwifery graduates achieved a **91 % passing rate** in the **November 15–16, 2024 Midwife Licensure Examination**, significantly higher than the **78.7 % national passing rate**.

The college community extends heartfelt congratulations to all newly licensed midwives. Their outstanding performance reflects the institution’s dedication to excellence in healthcare education and its vital role in preparing competent professionals for the nation.

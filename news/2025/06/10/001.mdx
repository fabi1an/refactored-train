The Veranza Activity Center at Veranza Mall, General Santos City, became a vibrant hub of talent and competition as the HRM Skills Olympics 2025 unfolded on March 8, 2025. The one-day event showcased the best of culinary and hospitality skills in a one-day competition that brought together aspiring professionals from different schools in the region.

Organized to highlight and enhance students' practical skills in hotel and restaurant management, the event featured a wide array of contests designed to test creativity, technique, and industry knowledge. Competitors faced off in categories such as table setting, breakfast challenge, market basket, cake decoration, grand flower arrangement, table napkin folding, food photography, food painting, fruit and vegetable carving, table skirting, virtual tour guiding, and bartending.

A highlight of the day was the presence of Senator Ronald “Bato” Dela Rosa, who attended as the guest of honor. He addressed the participants with an inspirational message encouraging them to pursue excellence in their chosen fields and contribute to the growth of the hospitality and tourism industry in the Philippines.

After a full day of competition, Goldenstate College of Malungon emerged as the overall champion, having delivered strong performances across multiple categories. The school’s consistent showing demonstrated not only the skill of its participants but also the quality of its HRM training programs.

The HRM Skills Olympics 2025 also served as a venue for collaboration between students, educators, and professionals. It allowed participants to experience real-world challenges while being assessed by industry practitioners, adding credibility and weight to their learning journey.

The event concluded on a high note, with organizers and guests expressing appreciation for the level of talent displayed. As the region continues to invest in tourism and service industries, platforms like the HRM Skills Olympics are seen as vital in preparing the next generation of hospitality professionals.

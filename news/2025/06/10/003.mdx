Goldenstate College of General Santos City celebrated its 33rd Commencement Exercises on May 7, 2025, at 4:00 PM at the Lagao Campus, marking a significant milestone for hundreds of graduates and their families.

The solemn and momentous occasion was graced by Hon. Lorelie G. Pacquiao, City Mayor of General Santos City, who served as the commencement speaker. In her address, Mayor Pacquiao praised the graduates for their determination and resilience, encouraging them to become agents of positive change in their respective communities.

Also present was Miss Chelsey Singson, third nominee of the Ako Ilocano Ako Partylist, who attended as guest of honor. In her message, she emphasized the power of education in empowering young Filipinos and shaping a stronger, more progressive nation.

The ceremony featured the formal presentation of diplomas, recognition of special awards, and honors for outstanding academic achievers. It was attended by faculty, parents, and local dignitaries, all gathered to celebrate the achievements and growth of the graduating class.

Goldenstate College reaffirmed its mission of producing well-rounded, competent professionals ready to meet the demands of a rapidly changing world, as it continues to play a vital role in the development of future leaders.

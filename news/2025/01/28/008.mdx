Goldenstate College Goes International: Builds Education Partnership in Vietnam.

Goldenstate College and Beyond Books Publication have formally launched a new international collaboration with the signing of a Memorandum of Understanding (MOU) on December 5, 2024, at Bach Viet Polytechnic in the heart of Ho Chi Minh City, Vietnam .

Leading the ceremony were Dr. Leonilo Capulso, President and CEO of Beyond Books Publication, and Dr. Warren A. Manilay, President and CEO of Goldenstate College. The agreement marks a shared commitment to elevate academic standards via collaborative initiatives.

The MOU establishes a framework for impactful joint efforts—from educational programs and resource-sharing to research collaborations—that aim to benefit not only both institutions but the broader global educational community.

By combining their expertise in publishing and higher education, Beyond Books Publication and Goldenstate College plan to break down barriers, spark innovation, and make significant contributions to worldwide education.

This partnership reflects the transformative outcomes when institutions across borders work together, setting the stage for future projects that promise to advance learning and empower educators and learners everywhere.

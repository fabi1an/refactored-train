Goldenstate College recently hosted Education Forum 2024, convening educators, students, and community stakeholders to critically examine the state and future of Philippine education.

The one-day forum fostered robust discussions on enhancing equity, inclusivity and effectiveness in the classroom. Speakers and participants exchanged ideas on best practices, identified reform priorities, and emphasized the power of collective dialogue in shaping national education policy.

Organizers confirmed that insights from the forum will be compiled into recommendations and shared with relevant policymakers, reinforcing the role of community engagement in driving educational change.

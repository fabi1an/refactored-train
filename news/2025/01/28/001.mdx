Goldenstate College of General Santos City’s College of Business and Marketing Management students seized the spotlight at the all‑out CBM Tindahan 2024, a business bazaar held during the GSCK Intramurals at the South Cotabato Sports Complex. The four‑day event featured hands‑on trading skills, as each BSBA batch vied for top sales performance.

Results were announced after an intense round of competition:

* **Champion** – BSBA 3 (Afternoon session)
* **1st Runner‑Up** – BSBA 3 (Morning session)
* **2nd Runner‑Up** – BSBA 1
* **3rd Runner‑Up** – BSBA 2
* **4th Runner‑Up** – BSBA 4

Organizers hailed the event as a showcase of creativity, collaboration, and business acumen, with strong turnout and enthusiastic community support.

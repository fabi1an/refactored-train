Goldenstate College of Maasim organized a one-day seminar on “Connecting Leadership and Communication” for Information Technology and Hospitality Management students. The event, held on November 25, 2024, featured Dr. Richard F. Fermocil, Dean of the College of Business and Management at Goldenstate College—General Santos City, as the keynote speaker.

Focusing on essential leadership principles and effective communication strategies, the seminar aimed to better prepare students for teamwork and professional success in their respective fields .

Goldenstate College continues its mission of empowering students through targeted, career-relevant training.

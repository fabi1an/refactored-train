Goldenstate College of Koronadal proudly congratulates Renz Espongja, an outstanding alumnus of the Hospitality Management program, on his recent promotion as Food and Beverage Coordinator at the prestigious Ramada Hotel in Abu Dhabi.

Renz’s promotion reflects his strong work ethic, professional commitment, and the solid foundation he received from Goldenstate College’s hospitality education. His achievement highlights the global readiness of the college’s graduates and the effectiveness of its hands-on training and industry-focused curriculum.

Goldenstate continues to shape future leaders equipped with the skills, knowledge, and confidence needed to excel in the dynamic world of hospitality. Renz’s success stands as a source of inspiration for current and future students.

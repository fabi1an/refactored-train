Goldenstate College made a strong impression at the 6th International Multidisciplinary Research Conference held on December 5–6, 2024, in Ho Chi Minh City, Vietnam, establishing itself as a rising force in global academic collaboration.

The college delegation included three distinguished leaders: Dr. Gregorio Warren A. Manilay, MNSA, Ed.D, Ph.D.; Dr. Josie Yap‑Tirador, D.B.A, Ph.D.; and Dr. Ferdinand G. Alcantara, Ed.D. 

Their active participation and presentations highlighted Goldenstate’s commitment to educational excellence and meaningful knowledge exchange on the international stage.

The conference, hosted by Beyond Books Publication in partnership with ISTAR‑Thailand, gathered scholars, researchers, and educators from diverse fields to present groundbreaking ideas and research findings.

By sharing their expertise and engaging with global peers, the delegates elevated Goldenstate College’s reputation as a leader in innovative education and academic research. Their contributions underscored the institution’s dedication to generating solutions that address real-world challenges.

Goldenstate College’s successful participation at this international conference reaffirms its mission to foster academic leadership and global collaboration for educators, students, and communities alike.

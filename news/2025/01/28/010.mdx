Hon. Rodante Marcoleta, a prominent legislator and staunch advocate for education reform, visited Goldenstate College at its Lagao Campus as part of his ongoing community outreach and educational advocacy efforts.

The event gathered students, faculty, and local leaders for a meaningful discussion on the critical role of education in national development. Marcoleta highlighted how education empowers individuals to become self-sufficient, make informed decisions, and contribute positively to society.

Accompanied by Dr. Warren A. Manilay, President and CEO, and Dr. Sarah Jane T. Manilay, Vice President for Finance, the visit underscored the college’s commitment to academic excellence and civic engagement.

During his address, Marcoleta shared insights from his years in public service and emphasized the importance of accessible, high-quality education, especially for underserved communities. He also outlined legislative initiatives aimed at expanding educational opportunities across the country.

The college administration expressed gratitude for Marcoleta’s visit, stating that his presence and message served as an inspiration to both students and educators.

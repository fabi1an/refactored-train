Goldenstate College made a notable mark at the 6th Multidisciplinary Conference for Awards and Research in Ho Chi Minh City, Vietnam, bringing home multiple honors that reinforce its commitment to excellence in education and research.

At the prestigious event, Dr. Warren A. Manilay, president and CEO of Goldenstate College, received the Outstanding Award for Leadership Educator in recognition of his visionary guidance and dedication to fostering a high-caliber academic environment.

The college’s scholarly strength was further highlighted by Dr. Josie Yap‑Tirador, vice president for academic affairs, who earned the title of Outstanding Distinguished Educator and placed third in the poster research category for her study, “Read with Me Program: An Inexpensive and Effective Approach in Teaching Children to Read.”

Also honored was Dr. Ferdinand G. Alcantara, school administrator of Goldenstate College–Koronadal City, who shared the Outstanding Distinguished Educator award and claimed third place as best presenter for his compelling presentation.

These accolades, won amid international competition, underscore Goldenstate College’s dedication to innovation and leadership in global education. The successful showing affirms its mission to support impactful research and nurture academic excellence.

Goldenstate College continues to elevate educators and students, building a reputation as a beacon of quality learning and positive community impact.

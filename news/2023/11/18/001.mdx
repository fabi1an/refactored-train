On behalf of the students, faculty, and staff and administration of goldenstate college, we would like to express our deepest gratitude to the officials of general santos city headed by mayor lorelie pacquiao, sanggunian panglungsod thru vice mayor lita nunez, pnp, afp reservists, jorge royeca hospital, soccsargen hospital, brgy capt. jaime tatad, and staff of barangay lagao for the invaluable assistance and support extended to our institution during the time of need.

Your prompt and unwavering response to our call for help has made a significant impact on the lives of our students. your dedication and commitment in providing immediate assistance have not only alleviated the challenges faced by our institution but have also given hope and reassurance to our students and their families. your dedication to the welfare of our students exemplifies the true essence of public service.

Once again, thank you for your support, timely assistance, and genuine concern for the students of goldenstate college.

With sincere appreciation,.

Sir warren and madam sara manilay.

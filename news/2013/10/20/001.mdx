# Goldenstate Intrams ’13: More Successful and Dazzling Than Ever

**GENERAL SANTOS CITY — Goldenstate College–Gensan wrapped up its five-day Intramurals 2013 with vibrant energy and unforgettable memories, marking one of the most dynamic celebrations in school history. Division 4 (BSHRM) emerged as overall champion, sweeping both sporting and pageant categories.**

Held from **September 23–27, 2013**, the annual semestral event took place at the **General Santos City Sports Complex** and **SM City Gensan Trade Hall**, blending intense competition with colorful showcases of talent, intellect, and school pride.

---

## 🏅 Highlights of the Event

- **🏆 Overall Champion**: Division 4 (BSHRM)
- **🎉 Grand Opening Parade & Cheerdance**: Division 3 dominated Cheerdance; Division 4 won the Parade and Banner Design.
- **🎭 Literary-Musical Events**: Division 4 shone in Newscasting and Pop Dance.
- **👑 Mr. & Ms. Goldenstate 2013**: Both winners hailed from Division 4.
- **💃 Mass Zumba Craze**: Thousands joined a synchronized Zumba performance — the largest in college history.

---

## 🎽 Sports & Games: Energy from Day One

The Intrams kicked off with a **colorful parade**, cheerleaders, and loud cheers from all four competing divisions. Competitions on **Day 1** included:

- **T-shirt Design** (🏆 Division 3)  
- **Banner Raising** (🏆 Division 3)  
- **Banner Design** (🏆 Division 4)  
- **Parade** (🏆 Division 4)  
- **Cheerdance** (🏆 Division 3)

In the afternoon, students clashed in major sports like **basketball, volleyball, softball, sepak takraw, chess, table tennis, and soccer**.

---

## 🎤 Day 2–3: Nonstop Action and Zumba Craze

**Day 2** brought more action as games continued with electric crowd hosting by **Madam Josie** and **Sir Allan**, who kept energy high while announcing results live.

A special moment capped **Day 3** with the **Zumba Dance Craze**, where students, faculty, and staff gathered in a unified, synchronized dance — a first in college history.

> 🧍 *“It was the biggest attended Zumba event Goldenstate has ever seen,”* one faculty member shared.

---

## 📚 Day 4: Battle of the Brains & Talents

For the first time, the **literary-musical events** were hosted at **SM City Gensan Trade Hall**, led by **Mr. Roger A. Pauya** and the **General Education Department**.

### Literary Events
- Quiz Bee  
- Spelling Bee  
- Extemporaneous Speaking  
- Newscasting

### Musical Events
- Vocal Solo & Duet  
- Pop Dance  
- Ballroom (Teacher & Student Categories)  
- Talent Showcase (Mr. & Ms. Goldenstate Candidates)

The venue was filled with tension and excitement as divisions battled with wit, creativity, and charm.

---

## 👑 Day 5: The Coronation of Goldenstate’s Finest

The grand finale came with the **Mr. & Ms. Goldenstate 2013 Pageant**, hosted by **Mr. Ferdinand Alcantara** and **Mr. Jay Costorio**. The stylish pageant was judged by esteemed jurors and supported by a high-spirited crowd.

- **👸 Ms. Goldenstate 2013** – Division 4  
- **🤴 Mr. Goldenstate 2013** – Division 4

Eight female and four male candidates competed, but Division 4 took both crowns in a surprise victory, affirming their overall dominance.

---

## 🏁 The Final Verdict

While every division gave their best, **Division 4** rose above the rest and was officially declared **Overall Champion** of the **2013 Goldenstate College–General Santos Intramurals**.
